Chocolate Chip Cookies

Instructions

  1. Before you start

    • Preheat Oven to 365*
    • Warm 1/2 stick of butter to room temperature
    • Gather all Measureing cups, mixing spoons, and bowl
      • 1/2 cup
      • 1 TBS
      • 1/4 Cup
      • 1/8 cup
      • 1/2 teaspoon

  2. Mixing the Treat

    1. Mix the wet goods in a bowl
      • 4 eggs
      • Warm butter
      • 3 cups Milk
      • Oil
    2. Mix the dry goods
      • 4 cups flour
      • TBS bakeing soda
      • 2 cups chocolate chips
      • 1/2 teaspoon sugar

  3. Prepare to Bake

    1. Spray metal bakeing sheet with oil
    2. Make 1x1x1 inch cookies
    3. Place 2 inch apart

  4. Bakeing

    1. Place cookie sheets in 365* Oven
    2. Wait 30
    3. Or until golden-brown
    4. Remove form oven and ...

  5. Enjoy
